Plee Preme: Banana Blossom Extract for Breastfeeding

Plee Preme for Maternal Breastfeeding is a safe banana blossom extract for mothers. We are the first and only original brand in Thailand that has undergone medical research and clinical testing. Awarded by the Department of Thai Traditional and Alternative Medicine, Ministry of Public Health, and recognized with over 10 national and international academic awards, we guarantee a quality that is different—from the taste to our specialized extraction process. Our formula is specifically developed to suit the maternal body with zero impact on infants, which remains the highest priority in our research and development process.

Plee Preme offers comprehensive, high-quality nutrition. We operate our own production facility supported by the National Innovation Agency (NIA) under the "Social Innovation" project. This allows us to optimize production costs while maintaining a perfect product that is delicious, safe, and nutritionally complete. Mothers can choose with confidence between our tasty ready-to-drink beverages and our convenient powder format for a warm, easy-to-mix drink—empowering mothers to create beautiful moments with their little ones every day.

 

Our Origin

Our story began when our founder faced the challenge of an insufficient milk supply and sought a milk-boosting solution that was affordable, had a long shelf life, and was free from drugs or medicinal herbs that could have long-term side effects. After rediscovering the traditional wisdom that banana blossoms can effectively support lactation, we became the pioneer in Thailand to conduct serious R&D on banana blossoms for breastfeeding. Since 2010, with over a decade of dedication, we have earned numerous quality assurance awards globally and received overwhelming positive feedback from consumers.

Currently, Plee Preme has expanded its reach internationally, with exports to Malaysia, Singapore, China, Dubai, Kuwait, the USA, and Japan.

Plee Preme Production Methods

Research Standards: Ready-to-Drink Products

  • Researched and developed by Assoc. Prof. Dr. Sittisin Bovornombat (Former Dean of the Faculty of Engineering and Agro-Industry, Maejo University), an expert in food microbiology from France.
  • Production processes for ready-to-drink products are directly supervised by Food Science faculty members.

Raw Materials

  • Based on current research and testing, we control production from the initial extraction stage. We maintain Contract Farming with farmers covering over 2,000 rai, with scheduled cultivation cycles to supply an average of 3 tons of raw materials per month.

Standard Criteria for Banana Blossom Raw Materials

  • Strictly regulated harvesting age for banana blossoms.
  • Precise active ingredient levels within defined average ranges. Every farm is tested before joining our network, followed by random inspections and soil sampling.
  • Strict control over cleaning methods and packaging preparation for delivery.
  • Scheduled logistics to ensure raw materials reach the factory within specified timeframes to prevent the degradation of essential active compounds, maintaining our high product standards.

Principles and Product Properties of Plee Preme

  • We prioritize nutritional safety and maternal physical recovery. Our research focuses deeply on extraction processes that ensure high bioavailability for easy absorption and safe excretion without side effects.
  • Flavonoid acid in our products aids in rapid postpartum recovery and helps reduce cellular inflammation throughout the body.
  • Plee Preme’s specialized powder extraction method helps mothers feel refreshed, reduces fatigue, improves emotional well-being, and promotes quality sleep—all of which contribute to high-quality breast milk.
  • Contains no drugs or other herbal additives. Herbal stimulation can lead to exhaustion from systemic overworking and may impact liver and kidney functions over long-term consumption. We believe herbal usage should remain under professional medical supervision.
  • Contains active compounds that reduce intestinal fat absorption, allowing the body to better absorb essential nutrients. This supports maternal health and optimizes Prolactin hormone function for efficient milk production.
  • Provides energy levels strictly based on nutritional research tailored to the specific caloric needs of breastfeeding mothers.
  • Delicious and safe; formulated without preservatives, artificial colors, or any chemical additives or enhancers.
  • High concentration: One bottle of Plee Preme Ready-to-Drink contains up to 95% banana blossom extract, with natural lemon and berry flavors.
  • Plee Preme is produced through a meticulous extraction process that preserves beneficial properties while removing potential toxins that could accumulate from continuous consumption. This differs from simple boiling and bottling; a difference in quality and texture that consumers can experience from the very first taste.

Clinical Research Summary
  • Objective: To investigate the effects of banana flower beverages on breast milk production among mothers with preterm neonates.
  • Methodology: A Randomized Controlled Trial (RCT) was conducted, comparing a treatment group (banana flower beverage) with a control group (plain water).
  • Results: Mothers in the treatment group showed significantly higher milk production than the control group on Day 2 and Day 3 postpartum.
  • Safety: No adverse effects were reported from the consumption of the banana flower beverage.
  • Conclusion: Banana flower is a promising galactagogue for augmenting breast milk, particularly for mothers at risk of insufficient milk supply due to preterm birth.
