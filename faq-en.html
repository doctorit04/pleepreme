Who is Pleepreme suitable for?

Pleepreme is suitable for breastfeeding mothers who wish to naturally support their wellness and breast milk production. Selected formulas are also suitable for pregnant women, as well as individuals looking for natural herbal beverages for daily health support.

Are Pleepreme products safe?

Pleepreme products are developed from natural ingredients, are stimulant-free, and produced under quality-controlled standards with safety as a priority. They are suitable for daily consumption when used as directed.

When is the best time to consume Pleepreme?

For breastfeeding mothers, it is recommended to consume Pleepreme approximately 15 minutes before breastfeeding. Products can generally be consumed 2–3 times per day, depending on the specific product instructions.

Which formula should I choose?

Pleepreme offers multiple formulas and formats to suit different needs, including instant powder, ready-to-drink beverages, and tea bag beverages, as well as options with ginger, low sugar, or sugar-free formulas. Mothers can choose based on personal preference and lifestyle. If unsure, consulting a healthcare professional is recommended.

What product formats are available from Pleepreme?

Pleepreme offers a variety of product formats to suit different lifestyles, including:

  • Instant soluble powder
  • Ready-to-drink beverages
  • Herbal tea bag beverages
